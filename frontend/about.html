About NovaCorp

Building the future of enterprise identity since 2019.

Our Mission

NovaCorp exists to eliminate the corporate password. We believe that decentralized identity — secured by cryptography and recorded on the blockchain — is the only way to build authentication systems that are truly trustworthy. Our platform puts the employee in control of their own identity while giving administrators complete, immutable visibility.

Our Story

From startup to enterprise platform in five years.

2019
Founded — Three engineers left enterprise security to build a better auth system from scratch using Web3 primitives.
2021
First Enterprise Client — Onboarded our first Fortune 500 client and processed over 1 million authentication events on-chain.
2024
Series A — Raised $12M to expand our team and build out the NovaCorp enterprise portal.
2026
Today — 50+ enterprise clients, 200,000+ employee authentications per month, zero data breaches.
